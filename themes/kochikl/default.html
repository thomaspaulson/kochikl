12 Jan - 15 Jan 2017

The British & Irish Lions are coming to Wellington for two not-to-be-missed matches against the Hurricanes and the All Blacks!

  • Book now
  • Add to itenary

One of the most significant sporting events since Rugby World Cup 2011, the nationwide DHL New Zealand Lions Series 2017 will feature two matches at Westpac Stadium:

Hurricanes v The British & Irish Lions (27 June 2017)
All Blacks v The British & Irish Lions (1 July 2017)

We all remember how the 2005 Series captivated New Zealand, and the 2017 Series won't be any different. Make sure you're there to see the best players from England, Ireland, Scotland and Wales face off against the All Blacks, the Maori All Blacks and the New Zealand Super Rugby franchises!
